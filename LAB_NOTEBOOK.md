# Laboratory Notebook

This notebook records the evolution of the laboratory.

It is not a diary.

It is a scientific notebook.

Each entry should answer questions such as:

- What did I learn?
- What surprised me?
- What did I implement?
- What difficulties did I encounter?
- What questions remain open?
- What ideas emerged?

The objective is to progressively build intuition and scientific reasoning.
